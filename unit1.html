Lesson #8 Color Images C.F.U

Which statement about analog and digital images is true?

Sampling an analog image more frequently produces a digital image with a better representation.

Describe how the process of sampling, RGB pixels, and binary sequences work together to display a digital color image.

Binary sequences, RBG pixels, and sampling all work together to display a digital color image by telling the computer where to put RBG pixels and how much opacity to apply to them. It is the culmination of these elements that produces a digital color image.
